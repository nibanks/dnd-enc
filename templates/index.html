D&D Beyond Settings

How to get your D&D Beyond cookies:

  1. Open dndbeyond.com and log in
  2. Press F12 to open Developer Tools
  3. Go to the Console tab
  4. Copy and paste this command: 
    document.cookie
  5. Press Enter and copy the result (it will look like a long text string)
  6. Paste it below and click Save Cookies

Note: Saving cookies will NOT scrape the monster list. Monster details will be fetched automatically when you add them to encounters.
