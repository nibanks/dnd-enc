How to get your D&D Beyond cookies:

  1. Open dndbeyond.com in your browser
  2. Log in to your D&D Beyond account (must be logged in!)
  3. Press F12 to open Developer Tools
  4. Go to the Console tab
  5. Copy and paste this command into the console: 
    document.cookie
  6. Press Enter and copy the entire result (it will be a long string with multiple cookies separated by semicolons)
  7. Paste it in the text box below and click Save Cookies
  8. If successful, you can now create adventures and add monsters!

Note: This gets all authentication cookies needed to access D&D Beyond content. Monster details are fetched on-demand when you add them to encounters.
